About the Programmes

As a business owner or a lender in the micro, small and medium enterprises (MSME) sector, you need access to finance to grow your business. But do you know how to get the best deals from banks and other financial institutions? Do you know how to use the latest schemes and products that are made for MSMEs? Do you know how to manage your cash flow and credit score well?

If you want to learn all this and more, then you should join the MSME Manthan, a unique event hosted by the Chamber of Indian Small Micro & Medium Enterprises. This event will give you a complete overview of the MSME financial ecosystem in India. You will get to interact with experts and peers from the top banks of India such as SBI, Punjab National Bank, Central Bank of India, and more.

The MSME Manthan is an event that you cannot afford to miss if you want to grow your business and achieve your financial goals. Don't miss this opportunity to network with other MSME stakeholders and gain valuable knowledge that will help you succeed. Register now and be a part of the MSME Manthan!

StartUp Glimpse

Chamber of Indian Micro Small & Medium Enterprises “CIMSME” prevails to promote & protect the trade & commerce of MSMEs in India. The MSME sector plays a very important role in overall economic growth of India. Understanding the importance of SME sector, CIMSME has taken proactive steps to spread awareness among the MSMEs for their conclusive growth by sharing the ideas and expertise. CIMSME is a Non-profit organization which represents interest of enterprises, particularly in MSME sector, with Banks; Financial Institutions; concerned Ministries and other Stakeholders, to promote bilateral dialogue for the benefit of MSMEs.
